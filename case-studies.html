University of Memphis

Transformed an existing campus store into the Ron Hart Foundation Market Nxt — a larger, more dynamic micro market in the cultural heart of the Maxine A. Smith Student Center. We grew the footprint and partnered with Compass Group’s Envision, Chartwells Higher Education Dining, and Spirit Architecture Group on the vision, folding in Amazon’s Just Walk Out technology for a seamless, modern grab-and-go experience the Tigers actually wanted.

Emory University

When Emory’s main café went offline for construction, students and staff still needed quality food across campus. We deployed six electric vehicles as satellite food-service units — supported by the main kitchen and positioned in high-traffic areas — keeping fresh, made-to-order meals within easy reach without the long walk.

SoFi Stadium
